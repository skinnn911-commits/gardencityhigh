Life at Garden City

Beyond the classroom: Discover your passions, build friendships, and grow as a leader.

A Nurturing Environment

Our campus is designed to be a "living laboratory." From the lush green spaces to our modern classrooms, every corner of Garden City High School supports the holistic development of our students.

We emphasize social awareness, civic responsibility, and environmental stewardship in everything we do.

Clubs & Organizations

Environmental Club

Join the "Green Team" in managing our school garden and promoting sustainable practices in Belmopan.

Athletics

Compete in football, basketball, and track. We focus on sportsmanship, discipline, and physical health.

Student Council

Develop leadership skills and represent your peers in school decision-making processes.

Arts & Culture

Express your creativity through music, drama, and visual arts that celebrate Belizean heritage.

Student Events

Enjoy group photos and school events that bring our community together.
