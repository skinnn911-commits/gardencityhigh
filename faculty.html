Our Mascot - PI

Meet our beloved school mascot! Our mascot name is PI in honour of the mathematical symnol that represents the concept of infinity, symbolizing the endless nature of existence and the universe. It embodies the spirit of Garden City High School and brings joy and enthusiasm to our school community. You'll find our mascot at school events, sports games, and celebrations throughout the year!
