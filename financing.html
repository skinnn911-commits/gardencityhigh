Financial Information

Investing in quality education is an investment in your child's future. Review our fee structure and payment options below.

Standard Fee Structure

The following table outlines the required fees for the 2026 academic year. All fees are in Belize Dollars (BZD).

Description Frequency Amount (BZD)
Registration FeeAnnual$150.00
Tuition (Government Grant Applied)Per Semester$250.00
Activity & Facilities FeeAnnual$100.00
Student ID CardOne-time$25.00
Science Lab FeeAnnual$50.00

*Activity fee and ID card fee are waived for Early Registration (Before Aug 31st).

Accepted Payment Methods

Bank Transfer

Payments can be made via Atlantic Bank or Belize Bank. Use the student's full name as the reference.

In-Person

Payments are accepted at the Administration Office via Cash or Card during regular school hours.

Government Assistance

As a partner with the Ministry of Education, Garden City High School accepts Education Vouchers. Parents are encouraged to apply through the Ministry's portal to help offset tuition costs.
