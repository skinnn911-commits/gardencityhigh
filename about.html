About Our Institution

Garden City High School is a co-educational institution located at #586 George Price Boulevard, Belmopan, Belize. We are committed to academic excellence, character development, and preparing students for success in a rapidly changing world.

Our Location

The school is centrally located and easily accessible to students, parents, and staff. Our campus provides a safe and convenient learning environment suitable for both academic and extracurricular activities.

Our Supportive Environment

At Garden City High School, we invite students and parents to become part of a community where innovation, discipline, and opportunity come together. Our campus isn't just a place of study; it's a fertile ground where potential is nurtured through:

  • Peer Mentorship: Older students guiding incoming First Forms.
  • Counseling Services: Emotional and academic support for every child.
  • Teacher Accessibility: Small class sizes for personalized attention.

"We don't just teach; we nurture."
